How AllInOneTrip makes you money 💸

AllInOneTrip is a metasearch launcher. You don't sell tickets — you send travelers to the sites that do, and earn affiliate commission when they book.

1
Traveler searches

They enter their trip once and hit "Search all sites."

2
Links carry your tag

Every provider link includes your affiliate ID (Travelpayouts, Booking, Expedia…).

3
They book anywhere

You get credited for the booking on the partner site — flights, hotels, cars, all of it.

4
You earn commission

Payouts land monthly. Add SEO content + social to grow the traffic that feeds it.
